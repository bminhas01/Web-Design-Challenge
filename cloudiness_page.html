Cloudiness (%)


The cloudiness level recorded on the day that this data was pulled - February 9th, 2021 - was one of the factors analyzed in this project. The graph below plots the cloudiness for each city in the dataset, against its latitude value. A city's relative position does not appear to affect its cloudiness levels. There is a weak linear relationship betweeen between the humidity values and latitude for cities in both the Northern Hemisphere (r value of 0.02) and Southern Hemisphere (r value of 0.11).
